About

Ever since childhood I have had a fascination with the way things operate. From bikes, to cars, to computers, I have used my observation and problem solving skills to overcome any obstacles in my way.

Software & Web Development

My software journey began in the late 90's building and repairing computers and designing 3D objects with Autocad. By the 2010s I had built a CNC machine (computer numeric control) and was designing with Solidworks and programming tool paths using G-code. I was also reprogramming computer chips for GM CPUs and using computers to diagnose and repair various automotive issues. During the pandemic I found time to explore software development and signed up for courses through Developer Direction. I enjoy the creativity and challenges that programming provide and am looking forward to growing and working as a developer.
